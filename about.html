About Us

Our Story

11.11 JEWELSS was born from a passion for creating beautiful, minimalist jewelry that complements any style. Our founder started designing pieces for friends and family, and the brand quickly grew as word spread about our unique designs and affordable luxury.

What began as a small Instagram shop has blossomed into a beloved brand with customers worldwide. Each piece is thoughtfully designed with clean lines and subtle embellishments, making them perfect for both everyday wear and special occasions.

We believe that jewelry should be accessible to everyone, which is why we focus on creating high-quality pieces at affordable prices. Our minimalist aesthetic ensures that our jewelry stands the test of time, both in durability and style.

Our Philosophy

The principles that guide our brand

Minimalism

We believe in the power of simplicity. Our designs focus on clean lines and subtle details that enhance rather than overwhelm. Each piece is crafted to be versatile and timeless.

Affordability

Luxury shouldn't come with a hefty price tag. We're committed to creating high-quality jewelry that's accessible to everyone, without compromising on materials or craftsmanship.

Trendy Yet Timeless

While we stay current with fashion trends, we design pieces that won't go out of style. Our jewelry is meant to be worn and loved for years to come, not just for a season.

Our Values

At 11.11 JEWELSS, we're committed to creating jewelry that not only looks beautiful but is also made with integrity. We carefully source our materials and work with skilled artisans to bring our designs to life.

We believe in building a community around our brand. Our customers aren't just buying jewelry; they're becoming part of the 11.11 JEWELSS family. We value your feedback and are constantly evolving to better serve your needs.

Sustainability is important to us. We're taking steps to reduce our environmental impact by using eco-friendly packaging and minimizing waste in our production process.
